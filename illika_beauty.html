Illika Beauty Lounge & Training Center was born from a passion for transformative beauty and the belief that every person deserves a sanctuary of care. Located in the heart of Birtamode, Jhapa, we offer deeply therapeutic and personalized treatments that restore balance, confidence, and radiance.

Beyond beauty services, we are committed to elevating the next generation of beauty professionals through our structured training programs — already nurturing over 1500 satisfied students.
